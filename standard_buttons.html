A general rule of thumb is, buttons are for actions and links are for navigation.

When a CTA has the importance of a button but is used for navigation rather than performing an action, use a link styled as a button.

The line height of button labels is intentionally larger than normal to ensure that buttons are the same height as similar targets, such as inputs.

Buttons may be disabled until prerequisite actions are taken on the page.
